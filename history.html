The Origin

"It began with a steady hand."

Founded by Ohiwan Lamar, our journey started not with heavy machinery, but in the realm of fine art. M/s Lamar Arts was born from the delicate strokes of commercial painting and the heavy, permanent craft of granite engraving.

Back then, there were no "Undo" buttons. Every letter on a memorial stone was hand-carved, and every signboard was hand-painted. This era established our standard for perfection—a standard we still hold today.

The Evolution

The Digital Architect

Handao Lamar

As the world moved forward, so did we. The transformation of Lamar Arts into a digital powerhouse was led by the visionary Handao Lamar, Ohiwan’s brother.

He bridged the gap between tradition and technology, introducing digital printing, flex, and modern design to Jowai. Tragically, we lost Handao in 2019. He left behind not just a business, but a legacy of innovation. Today, that torch has been passed back to his brother, Ohiwan, and the next generation.
